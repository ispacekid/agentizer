## What Is This?

**Agentizer** is a satirical skill that does the exact opposite of [humanizer](https://github.com/blader/humanizer). Instead of making AI-generated text sound more human, it makes *any* text sound **more like AI** — absurdly, hilariously more.

It's a comedy tool. A critique. A mirror held up to the formulaic, buzzword-heavy, em-dash-loving, "It's worth noting that"-spouting style that AI models default to.

**This is not for evading AI detectors.** It's for laughing at them.

## Features

- **`/agentizer-on`** — Activate persistent AI mode. Every response from your agent will be drenched in AI-isms until you say `/agentizer-off`.
- **`/agentizer-off`** — Deactivate. Return to normal human speech.
- **`/agentizer [text]`** — One-shot rewrite. Paste any text and get back the most AI-flavored version possible.

## How It Works

Agentizer applies a five-step AI-ification process to every response:

1. **The Hook** — Sycophantic opener ("Great question!")
2. **The Promise** — No-fluff commitment ("Let me break this down")
3. **The Breakdown** — Over-explained content with AI vocabulary
4. **The Tech-Metaphor Stack** — Quantum this, paradigm that
5. **The Open-Ended Closer** — "Would you like me to dive deeper?"

The vocabulary, phrases, and style rules are maintained in `references/` for easy updating.
